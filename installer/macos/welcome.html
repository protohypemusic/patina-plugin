Patina

Version 1.0.0

A lo-fi texture plugin that adds character, warmth, and grit to any sound.

  • Noise — Vinyl crackle and tape hiss
  • Wobble — Volume tremolo with adjustable rate
  • Distort — Warm saturation to aggressive drive
  • Space — Lush reverb with decay control
  • Flux — Beat-repeat glitch effects
  • Filter — High-pass and low-pass shaping
